GlowShift Fruits Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how GlowShift Fruits (“the app”) handles information when you use the game on iPhone or iPad.

Information We Collect

GlowShift Fruits does not require account creation and does not directly collect personal information such as your name, address, or email address inside the app.

The app stores gameplay-related information locally on your device, including:

  • best scores by difficulty mode
  • game settings such as sound, music, haptics, and difficulty
  • booster inventory and purchase-related unlock state

Game Center

GlowShift Fruits supports Apple Game Center for leaderboards. If you choose to sign in to Game Center, leaderboard scores may be submitted through Apple’s Game Center services. Game Center is operated by Apple and is subject to Apple’s own terms and privacy practices.

In-App Purchases

GlowShift Fruits offers optional in-app purchases, such as ad removal and booster packs. Purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store. The app does not store your payment card information.

Advertising

The current version of GlowShift Fruits does not use a live third-party advertising SDK for rewarded ads. If advertising features are added in a future version, this Privacy Policy will be updated before release.

How Information Is Used

Local gameplay data is used only to:

  • save your progress-related settings
  • restore purchased gameplay benefits inside the app
  • show your scores and leaderboard progress

Data Sharing

The app does not sell your personal information. Data may be shared only through Apple services that you choose to use, such as Game Center or the App Store purchase system.

Data Retention

Gameplay settings and local progress data remain on your device until you remove the app or clear the app’s data by deleting it.

Children’s Privacy

GlowShift Fruits is designed as a general audience game and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated if the app’s features or data practices change. The latest version should always be published at the privacy policy URL listed on the App Store product page.

Contact

If you have privacy questions about GlowShift Fruits, contact the developer at your support email or support page listed on the App Store.
